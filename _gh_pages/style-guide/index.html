Nomenclature and Messaging

Classmates® Nomenclature

Nomenclature

In consumer facing materials, the name of our service is Classmates®. After the first usage in text on a page or marketing vehicle you can drop the ® bugging.
Do Not:

  • Refer to the service as Classmates.com
  • Refer to service as Memory Lane
  • Use the word “classmates” as a generic term for people in a class. Instead you can use: “class members” “people in your class” “people from high school”

Possessive is OK
Classmates’ is OK, but it is preferred to write as “blank by Classmates.”

Tagline

You high school class, all in one place.

Service Description - Short

With the largest directory of high school classes and digitized yearbooks available anywhere, Classmates makes finding, reconnecting and staying up with your class fun and easy.

Service Description - Long

Classmates® is the online home for your high school class. With the largest directory of high schools and class lists available anywhere, Classmates makes finding, reconnecting and staying up with your high school class fun and easy. Classmates also has over 170,000 digitized high school yearbooks available for you to view and share photos.

Business Description

Classmates operates the leading online service in the U.S. for bringing high school classes together. With class lists that cover over 57 million registered accounts and include maiden names, Classmates makes it easy for its users to find and reconnect with high school friends. Classmates features the largest library of digitized yearbooks, with over 170,000 available to browse online, so users can tag and share old photos from school.
Check the Counts!
If you use Yearbooks number check with the Yearbooks team to make sure it is current.
